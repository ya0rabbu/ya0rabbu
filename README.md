### 👋 About Me

I'm a **UI/UX Designer & Product Manager** based in **Dhaka, Bangladesh**.

Currently serving as **Team Lead at Softvance / FSD Agency**, overseeing 85+ members across UI/UX, Frontend, Flutter, AI, Back-End, and Laravel departments — managing the full design-to-delivery pipeline.

On the freelance side, I deliver high-quality design and development work on Fiverr under **[ui-panda](https://www.fiverr.com/ui-panda)** and **[skylork](https://www.fiverr.com/skylork)** — projects spanning fintech dashboards, healthcare platforms, legal SaaS, real estate, food & hospitality, and e-commerce.

- 🎯 Specialties: Product thinking, design systems, Figma-to-code, React/Next.js, Webflow
- 🏗️ Recent work: InsurTech platforms, EdTech apps, Flutter finance apps, DOOH advertising tools
- 📍 Dhaka, Bangladesh · Muslim · Always building
